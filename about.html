Our Story

Built on a promise made to one family

Elite Care began when our founder struggled to find caregivers who treated her own father with the patience and respect he deserved. What started as a small, personal solution grew into a mission: to give every family access to the kind of care we'd want for our own.

Today, our caregivers support individuals across the region — not as a rotating shift of strangers, but as familiar, trusted faces who know each client's routine, preferences, and personality.
